Submission Form

Have a poem, story, or essay you'd like to share? Submit your work to be featured in our monthly newsletter.Please ensure you provide all necessary details for us to properly review and potentially publish your submission.

All submissions will be reviewed by our editorial team. We'll contact you if your work is selected for publication.

Please allow 2-3 weeks for our team to review your submission.

What Can You Submit?

INVICTUS welcomes a variety of creative and literary works from students. Here are some examples of what you can submit:

Creative Writing

  • poems
  • Short stories & fiction
  • essays & memoirs
  • Plays & screenwriting excerpts
  • Creative non-fiction

Articles & Opinion Pieces

  • Campus news & events
  • Social commentary
  • Political analysis
  • Educational topics
  • Cultural reviews

Visual & Other Arts

  • artwork (digital or scanned)
  • Comics & illustrations
  • Cartoons & caricatures
  • Designs & graphics

Submission Guidelines: Include your name, course, and contact information. Written submissions should be in word,pdf or any other text files. For visual art, ensure images are high quality.

Submission Process: Click the "Open Submission Form" button below to access our Google Form and submit your work.
